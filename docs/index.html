Continuum

Continuum is a local-first publishing and identity environment built for durable authorship and digital sovereignty.

Instead of publishing directly from a web platform, Continuum allows you to manage your identities, sign events locally, and maintain your own archive of published work.

Core Ideas

  • Local-first software
  • Self-custody of keys
  • Durable authorship
  • Intentional publishing

What You Can Do

  • Manage multiple Nostr identities
  • Publish notes and long-form articles
  • Maintain a durable local archive
  • Sign events locally before publishing
