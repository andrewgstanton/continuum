Who it’s for

Continuum is for people who want control over what they write and publish.

If you are happy using Word, Google Docs, Substack, or a website and do not think much about where your work lives, you probably do not need Continuum.

But if you are tired of splitting your work across documents, apps, and platforms, and want everything in one place that you can run and keep yourself, Continuum will make sense.
