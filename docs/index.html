Continuum

A local-first publishing and identity environment for durable authorship and digital sovereignty.

Continuum allows you to manage identities, write content, sign events locally, and maintain a permanent archive of your work.

Download Continuum

Local First

Continuum runs on your own machine. Your identities, keys, and publishing workflow remain under your control.

Self-Custodied Identity

Manage multiple Nostr identities and sign events locally before publishing them to relays.

Durable Archive

All content is stored locally as part of your archive, ensuring your work remains durable and independent of any platform.
