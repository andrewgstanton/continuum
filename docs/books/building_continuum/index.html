About the Book

Building Continuum: Local Authority in a Networked World is a short book about digital sovereignty, authorship, and why your writing should not depend entirely on platforms you do not control.

This is not a software manual. It explains the deeper thinking behind Continuum and the kind of work it is meant to support.

The book explores local-first publishing, identity, memory, and the importance of keeping durable authority over your own work.
