Continuum Overview

Continuum is a writing and publishing workspace you run on your own machine. It helps keep your work and the way you publish it under your control.

Local First

Continuum runs locally on your own machine, so your writing and publishing workflow stay under your control. Your work does not depend on another platform to exist.

Write and publish under your own name

Continuum allows you to manage multiple publishing profiles and choose how your work is shared. Whether you write under one name or several, the workflow stays in your hands.

How it works

Instead of depending on a centralized service, Continuum lets you create and prepare your work locally before choosing when and where to publish it.

  • Write locally
  • Prepare your work
  • Choose when to publish

Your work stays with you

All of your published work is stored locally as part of your own long-term record. This helps ensure that your writing remains available and independent of any single platform.

Getting Started

To begin using Continuum:

  1. Choose a package from the downloads page
  2. Install the version that fits your system
  3. Run the included startup script or application
  4. Open Continuum locally
