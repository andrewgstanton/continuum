Continuum Pricing

Continuum is distributed as local-first software. You download the package, run it on your own machine, and retain custody of your identities and data.

There is no required subscription or hosted account.

Local-First Model

Continuum is not a hosted platform — it is software you run yourself.

This approach keeps your keys, identities, and publishing workflow under your control.

You can download the software from the downloads page and run it locally.
